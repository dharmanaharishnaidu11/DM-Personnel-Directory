5 View Mode — Tree Structures

How each view mode reorganizes the same personnel data into different tree hierarchies

1. Department View (Default)

  • ▼Sectore.g., Executive & Revenue Admin
    • ▼Categorye.g., Revenue & District Administration
      • ▼Districte.g., Krishna
        • PersonName, Designation

Map: Color-coded by 8 sectors. Use: "Show all Fire dept personnel across AP"

2. Geographic View

  • ▼Districte.g., Prakasam
    • ▼Mandale.g., Ongole
      • ▼Categorye.g., Police
        • PersonName, Designation

Map: Admin boundary emphasis, color by category. Use: "Who is posted in Prakasam district?"

3. ESF View

  • ▼ESF Numbere.g., ESF-4: Search & Rescue
    • ▼Lead Agencye.g., SDRF
      • PersonName, Designation
    • ▼Supporting Agencies
      • PersonName, Designation

Map: Color by primary ESF (16 colors). Use: "Who handles ESF-4 Search & Rescue?"

4. Chain of Command View

  • ▼Level 1 — Apexe.g., Chief Secretary
    • ▼Level 2 — Dept Heade.g., MD APSDMA
      • ▼Level 3 — Districte.g., Collector, Krishna
        • ▼Level 4 — Sub-Districte.g., RDO, Machilipatnam
          • ▼Level 5 — Mandale.g., Tahsildar
            • Level 6 — Villagee.g., VRO

Map: Graduated color by hierarchy level. Use: "Show reporting chain from CS down through Revenue"

5. Disaster Duty View

  • ▼Disaster / Evente.g., Cyclone DANA — Oct 2025
    • ▼Duty Statuse.g., On Duty
      • ▼Team / Groupe.g., SDRF Team Alpha
        • PersonName, Duty Location, Shift

Map: Color by duty status (On Duty=green, Standby=blue). Use: "During a cyclone, who is deployed where?"

About APSDMA

The Andhra Pradesh State Disaster Management Authority (APSDMA) is the apex body for disaster management in the state, established under the Disaster Management Act, 2005.
